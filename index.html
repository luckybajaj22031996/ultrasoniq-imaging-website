About UltraSoniq

Where Advanced Technology Meets Compassionate Radiology

UltraSoniq Imaging and Interventions is a specialised sports injury imaging & pain clinic in Bhopal, led by Dr. Suvinay Saxena and Dr. Satya Jha — a husband-wife team of fellowship-trained radiologists with combined expertise spanning musculoskeletal imaging, women's health, and image-guided interventions.

What We Offer
Specialised Radiology Services

Comprehensive diagnostic imaging and image-guided interventions, delivered by sub-specialty trained radiologists.

Musculoskeletal Imaging

Advanced MRI, CT, high-resolution ultrasound, and dynamic imaging for joints, bones, soft tissues, and peripheral nerves. Precision diagnosis for sports injuries and chronic conditions.

Dr. Suvinay

Image-Guided Interventions

Minimally invasive procedures including joint injections, PRP therapy, calcification barbotage, ACL decompression, and regenerative treatments under imaging guidance.

Dr. Suvinay

Women's Imaging

Specialised imaging for women's health including breast imaging, gynaecological ultrasound, and comprehensive women-specific diagnostic evaluations.

Dr. Satya

Fetal Imaging

Detailed fetal ultrasound and diagnostic imaging for pregnancy monitoring, anomaly screening, and prenatal health assessments with expert interpretation.

Dr. Satya

Abdominal & Onco-Imaging

Comprehensive imaging for liver, biliary, pancreatic, renal pathologies, and oncological assessments including musculoskeletal tumours and cancer staging.

Both Doctors

Sports Injury Management

End-to-end care for athletes — from precise diagnosis of sports-related injuries to image-guided treatments and rehabilitation support for full recovery.

Dr. Suvinay
